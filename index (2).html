What is a
Percent?

Per-cent is a fancy way to say “out of 100.” The little sign % is just shorthand for the words “per hundred.” If 60% of people voted, that means 60 out of every 100 people voted.

Why 100? 🤔

Because 100 is friendly! It splits neatly and it’s easy to picture.

The golden rule:
A percent is a fraction whose bottom number (denominator) is always 100.
78%  =  78100    and    53100  =  53%
Half a pizza 🍕 = 50%. Cut a pizza into 100 crumbs — half of them is 50 crumbs, which is 50 out of 100 = 50%.

For grown-ups

🍎 Teaching Notes

Start concrete, then abstract

Every abstract rule (“divide by 100”) should be introduced after the hundred-square picture. Let the child colour 30 boxes and see that “30 out of 100” and “30%” are the same thing before writing 30/100.

The one sentence that unlocks everything

“Percent means out of 100.” Ask the child to say it aloud before each problem. To convert a fraction to a percent, we simply rename it so the bottom becomes 100. 4/5 = 80/100 = 80%.

Teach “of” means multiply

When a question says “80% of 15,” the word of is the signal to multiply: (80 ÷ 100) × 15. Highlight the word “of” in yellow every time it appears.

Common mistakes to watch for 👀

✗ Wrong: Writing 4/5 = 4% (just copying the top number).
✓ Fix: Make the bottom 100 first → 80/100 = 80%.
✗ Wrong: 0.53 = 0.53% (forgetting to move the point).
✓ Fix: Multiply by 100 → move the decimal 2 places → 53%.
✗ Wrong: For “broken eggs”, giving the number instead of a percent, or the wrong base.
✓ Fix: Always ask “out of what total?” — that total is the denominator.
✗ Wrong: Reducing 32% to 32/10 or forgetting to simplify.
✓ Fix: 32% = 32/100, then divide top & bottom by 4 → 8/25.

Quick reference card 🗂️

Fraction → % :   multiply by 100  →  45 × 100 = 80%
% → Fraction :   put over 100 & simplify  →  32% = 32100 = 825
Decimal → % :   move point 2 places right  →  0.53 = 53%
% → Decimal :   move point 2 places left  →  20% = 0.2
% of a number :   (% ÷ 100) × number  →  80% of 15 = 12
Score as % :   (score ÷ total) × 100  →  195/300 × 100 = 65%
